Donate Now and Pay It Forward

We’ve designed a unique student loan program to help our students get the funding needed to become awesome UX design professionals. This program, which we call the Give Forward Student Loan Fund, is powered by contributions from us, partner corporations, folks in our local community, and people like you that want to see our industry improve by empowering those interested in making things better.

How will my donation be used?

Your entire donation (minus the fees PayPal charges us) goes directly into the Give Forward Student Loan Fund.

What exactly is the Give Forward Student Loan Fund?

The Give Forward Student Loan Fund is a nonprofit fund that allows qualifying students to borrow what they need to pay for tuition at Center Centre. In addition to borrowing up to the full tuition cost ($59,880), students can also borrow an additional $20,000 per year of our two-year program to help cover living expenses, which goes a long way in Chattanooga.

We’ve intentionally designed these loans to focus on our students first. Each loan is interest-free while a student is enrolled in our program and actively taking courses. Interest doesn’t start to accrue until 90 days after a student graduates.

The interest rate on the loan is low and fixed. Once repayment begins, graduates may take up to 20 years to pay off the loan, keeping monthly payments manageable. As students pay these loans back, their payments go right back into the fund for use by future students.

Who services the loan fund?

The Give Forward Student Loan Fund is serviced through Chattanooga Neighborhood Enterprise (CNE). We chose CNE because of their extensive experience in gracious approaches to lending.

CNE builds relationships with their customers, meaning that our students will get to know their lending servicers by name. Students that borrow from the Give Forward Student Loan Fund will work directly with CNE, getting the flexibility needed to handle unforeseen challenges making a payment or adjusting their loan.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

CNE is an approved 501(c)(e) nonprofit corporation, but we cannot advise you on any tax questions about your contribution. Please consult with your tax advisor regarding the proper handling of this gift on your tax return, or for any tax-related questions.

  1. Donate to the Give Forward Student Loan Fund:
    use this button; you’ll be taken to a PayPal page where you can donate as much as you'd like, either paying with your PayPal account or the debit/credit card of your choice.
  2. Spread the word about what we’re doing:
    tell your friends, families, colleagues, and everyone that might be interested in what we're trying to accomplish. We can’t do this alone! Share this page on Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, or by email.
